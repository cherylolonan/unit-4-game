Chaos Emeralds Collector!

Sonic, you will be given a random number by Dr. Robotnik at the start of the game.

There are four Chaos Emeralds below. By clicking on one you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to that of Dr. Robotnik's, but you lose if your total score goes above Eggman's.

The value of each Chaos Emerald is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Chaos Emerald.
